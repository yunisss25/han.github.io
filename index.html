Pikachu

Pikachu is a small, yellow, mouse-like Pokémon that has become one of the most iconic characters in the Pokémon franchise. Its body is round and petite, with brown stripes across its back, and its large, expressive black eyes and cheerful smile give it an endearing look. Pikachu’s cheeks are bright red, a feature that stores electricity, and its long, pointed ears have black tips. Its tail is shaped like a lightning bolt, further emphasizing its electric powers.

Pokemon

Pikachu is known for its playful, loyal nature, often seen as the companion of Ash Ketchum in the Pokémon anime series. It stands about 1'4" (40 cm) tall and can generate and release electricity through its moves like Thunderbolt, which has become a signature ability of the character. Pikachu’s combination of cuteness and electric powers has made it a beloved and enduring symbol of the Pokémon franchise.
